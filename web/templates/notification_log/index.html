Notification Log

Record of all notifications sent by gniza across all channels.

Table columns

  • Date — When the notification was sent.
  • Channel — Delivery channel (email, telegram, webhook, ntfy, healthcheck).
  • Status — OK (delivered) or FAIL (send error).
  • Destination — Recipient address, chat ID, or URL.
  • Subject — Notification subject line.

Tips

  • Use Settings → Notifications to configure channels and send test notifications.
  • FAIL entries indicate the send failed — check channel settings and connectivity.
